Are Window Treatments Really Worth the Investment For Oklahoma City Area Homeowners?

At Shaded In The Sun here in the Oklahoma City area, I often hear the question: "Are window treatments really worth the money?" It's a valid question, and the short answer is a resounding yes! While they might seem like purely decorative elements, high-quality window treatments offer a multitude of benefits that go far beyond just aesthetics for your Oklahoma City home.

Let's delve into the real value and why investing in the right window coverings can be one of the smartest decisions you make for your property:

1. Enhanced Energy Efficiency & Cost Savings:

Oklahoma City summers are scorching, and our winters can bring unexpected chills. Windows are often the biggest source of heat gain in the summer and heat loss in the winter. Properly installed window treatments like cellular shades, or insulated curtains act as a barrier, helping to regulate your home's temperature.

  • • Summer: They block out intense sunlight, reducing the need to crank up your air conditioner, leading to significant savings on your energy bills.
  • • Winter: They trap warm air inside, preventing it from escaping through the glass, reducing your reliance on your heating system.

Over time, the energy savings alone can make high-quality window treatments a worthwhile investment.

2. Unparalleled Light Control & Privacy:

Whether you want to flood your living room with natural light or create a dark and cozy atmosphere for a movie night, the right window treatments give you complete control.

  • • Light Filtering: Options like sheer shades or light-filtering roller shades diffuse harsh sunlight, reducing glare while still allowing natural light to brighten your space. This is perfect for living areas and kitchens.
  • • Room Darkening & Blackout: They trap warm air inside, preventing it from escaping through the glass, reducing your reliance on your heating system.
  • • Winter: They trap warm air inside, preventing it from escaping through the glass, reducing your reliance on your heating system.

3. Protecting Your Interiors:

The Oklahoma sun, while beautiful, can be damaging to your furniture, flooring, and artwork over time. UV rays can cause fading and deterioration. Investing in window treatments with UV protection can significantly prolong the life of your interior furnishings, saving you money on replacements in the long run.

4. Elevating Your Home's Aesthetics & Value:

Let's not forget the visual impact of well-chosen window treatments. They can dramatically enhance the look and feel of any room, adding a touch of style, sophistication, and personality.

  • • Customization: With a wide array of fabrics, materials, colors, and styles available, you can perfectly tailor your window treatments to complement your existing décor and personal taste.
  • • Luxury Feel: Upgrading to custom or high-end window treatments can instantly elevate the perceived value and luxury of your home, which is a significant factor if you ever plan to sell.

5. Increased Comfort & Functionality:

Beyond the tangible benefits, the right window treatments simply make your home more comfortable and functional. They can reduce noise pollution from busy Oklahoma City streets, improve insulation for a cozier atmosphere, and provide ease of use with motorized options for hard-to-reach windows.

In Conclusion:

For Oklahoma City area homeowners, window treatments are far more than just window coverings. They are a valuable investment that offers significant returns in terms of energy savings, light and privacy control, protection of your belongings, enhanced aesthetics, and overall comfort.


Before you decide, consider your specific needs and the unique climate of Oklahoma City. By choosing the right custom or luxury window treatments, you're not just buying a product; you're investing in the long-term comfort, value, and enjoyment of your home.
