Convenience

The ability to adjust your shades to your desired position with a simple voice command offers an unparalleled level of convenience. Imagine adjusting your shades without leaving the couch. The ease of control and adjustment from anywhere in the room or even the world adds a layer of luxury and simplicity to your daily life.

Scheduling

Motorized shades can be scheduled for automatic operation, providing hands-free adjustment at any time of the day. Program them to open with the sunrise and close at sunset, or at specific times to increase energy efficiency or give the illusion that you're still home.

Energy Efficiency

Managing sunlight throughout the day can significantly increase your home's energy efficiency. By automatically adjusting your shades to block out the sun during peak hours, you can reduce your reliance on air conditioning, leading to lower energy bills and a more sustainable lifestyle.

Safety

Opting for motorized window treatments enhances safety by getting rid of cords. This is especially important in homes with small children or pets, eliminating the risk of entanglement and creating a safer environment for your loved ones.

Integration

For seamless control, Graber motorized systems can often be integrated into numerous different smart home systems. Connect your window treatments with other smart devices, such as lighting, thermostats, and security systems, to create a fully automated and responsive home environment.

Hard-to-Reach Windows

Smart shades are a practical solution for windows that are difficult to access. Whether it's a tall window, one behind furniture, or simply one that's inconvenient to reach, motorization eliminates the need for manual adjustments.

By incorporating smart home technology with Graber Blinds, you can create a space that is stylish, functional, and convenient.
