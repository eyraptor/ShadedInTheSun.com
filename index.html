Motorization

Motorized blinds and shades offer the highest level and ease and convenience. Not only does motorization improve safety by removing unsightly cords, it allows you to operate your shades—hands free—from any room in your home, or from halfway around the world with use of a hub. Motorized shades can be scheduled for automatic operation. They can also be integrated into more than a dozen different smart home systems.

UltraLite Cordless Lift System

The UltraLite cordless lift system from Graber takes the cordless experience to a whole new level. It is ultra-smooth, whisper quiet, and remarkably precise. Raise and lower your solar, roller, and layered shades with the lightest touch—with so little noise that it won’t wake a sleeping baby. Move the shade exactly where you want with almost no effort. And because you can place your shade precisely every time, you can easily adjust the shade to get the ideal light and privacy you desire. This is the ultimate cordless experience—one that you need to try to believe.
