### Source 1: Graber (graberblinds.com/why-graber/window-cord-safety/)
- Free-hanging cords pose strangulation hazards to children and pets
- Continuous-loop pull cords create accessible loops = dangerous
- Cordless lift systems eliminate free-hanging cords
- Motorized lift options available
- Retractable-cord designs minimize accessible cord length
- Tension devices keep continuous-loop cords taut
- **"Best for Kids" certification** = no cords or inaccessible inner cords
- Third-party lab testing required for certification
- Window Covering Safety Council recommends cordless in children's bedrooms
