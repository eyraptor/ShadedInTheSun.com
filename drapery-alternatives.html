SmartDrape™: A Modern Take on Drapery Innovation

For those who love the soft, flowing look of drapery but seek modern functionality, SmartDrape™ shades offer a revolutionary solution. These vertical sheer shades are designed to provide both elegance and unparalleled versatility.

  • • Soft Drapery Aesthetic: SmartDrape™ features undulating sheer and fabric vanes that create a delicate, architectural look reminiscent of soft-fold drapery.
  • • Unique Walk-Through Design: One of its most innovative features is the individual vane construction that allows you to literally walk through the shade, even when it's closed! This makes them ideal for patio doors and wide windows, seamlessly connecting your indoor and outdoor living spaces.
  • • Dual Opacity Control: Easily switch between a sheer view for clear illumination and a light-filtering or room-darkening setting with a simple twist or slide of a wand.
  • • Modern Appeal & Safety: Available in on-trend whites, off-whites, and grays, with sophisticated hardware finishes like Brass and Bronze, SmartDrape™ enhances any contemporary space. Plus, they are cordless and "Best for Kids" certified, ensuring safety for your family.
  • • Motorization Options: For ultimate convenience, SmartDrape™ can be motorized, allowing you to control your shades with the touch of a button via a remote or smartphone app.

Roman Shades: Classic Elegance with Modern Convenience

Roman shades beautifully combine the soft aesthetic of drapery with the practical features of blinds. Imagine fabric panels that elegantly fold into neat horizontal pleats when raised, and then lie flat for a clean, tailored appearance when lowered.

Roman Shades

  • • Versatile Styles: Whether you prefer a structured look with flat folds and batten backs, a seamless flat fold without visible seams, or a more traditional cascading 'hobbled' style, Roman shades offer diverse design options to match any decor.
  • • Fabric Choices: From natural and breathable cotton and linen to easy-care poly blends, you can select materials that fit your lifestyle and desired level of light filtration.
  • • Advanced Operation: Experience ultimate convenience with cordless systems for child and pet safety, or opt for motorized Roman shades that can be controlled with a remote, voice commands, or integrated into your smart home system.
  • • Benefits: Beyond their beauty, Roman shades offer excellent light control, enhanced privacy, and even contribute to energy efficiency by providing an insulating layer at your windows.

Roller Shades

  • • Contemporary Design: Roller shades provide a minimalist look that complements modern interiors, available in hundreds of fabrics, colors, textures, and opacities.
  • • Light Control Options: Choose from sheer fabrics to gently filter light and maintain your view, light-filtering options for privacy and diffused light, or room darkening/blackout fabrics to block nearly 100% of incoming light—perfect for bedrooms or media rooms.
  • • Innovative Features: Look for systems like the PrecisionLift™ cordless operation for smooth, quiet adjustments, or the LightGuard 360™ system for ultimate room darkening by minimizing light seepage around the edges.
  • • UV Protection: Soluna™ Solar Shades are designed to protect your furniture, flooring, and artwork from harmful UV rays while still allowing you to enjoy your outdoor view.

Cellular Shades

  • • Superior Insulation: This trapped air creates an insulating barrier that helps keep your home cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter. This can lead to significant savings on your utility bills, as windows are a major source of heat gain or loss.
  • • Flexible Light Control: Norman's Portrait™ honeycomb line offers options from sheer to light filtering to room darkening/blackout, giving you complete control over the ambiance and privacy of your space.
  • • Sound Absorption: A standout benefit of honeycomb shades is their ability to dampen noise, making them an excellent choice for bedrooms, living rooms, or any area where quiet comfort is desired.
  • • Modern Operation: Enjoy the convenience and safety of SmartRise cordless systems, SmartFit™, or motorized options for effortless control.

Find Your Perfect Window Treatment

From the tailored elegance of Roman shades to the insulating power of cellular shades, the sleek simplicity of roller shades, and the innovative walk-through design of SmartDrape™, there are many fantastic alternatives to traditional drapery.

Each option offers unique benefits in terms of style, light control, privacy, and energy efficiency, helping you create the perfect atmosphere in every room of your home.
