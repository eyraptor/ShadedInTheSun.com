Shades and Shutters and Blinds, Oh My!
Choosing the Perfect Window Coverings for Your Home

Call Brent & Edna today at 405-259-5599
for your window treatment questions.

At Shaded In The Sun, we understand that selecting the right window coverings for your Edmond or Oklahoma City home can feel overwhelming. With so many options available – from classic blinds to stylish shutters – it's easy to get lost in the sea of choices.

1. Consider Your Needs and Lifestyle

  • Privacy: Do you need complete darkness for sleeping or just a little extra privacy during the day?

  • Energy Efficiency: Are you looking to reduce energy costs by keeping your home cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter?

  • Light Control: How much sunlight do you want to let in? Do you need to block glare from your TV or computer screen?

  • Aesthetics: What style best complements your home's décor? Do you prefer a modern, minimalist look or something more traditional?

  • Budget: Set a realistic budget before you start shopping. Window treatments can range in price significantly.

2. Explore the Popular Options

  • Blinds:

    • Vertical Blinds: Ideal for large windows and sliding glass doors

    • Horizontal Blinds: A classic and affordable option, available in various materials like wood, aluminum, and faux wood.

  • Shades:

    • Roller Shades Simple and sleek, offering various light filtering and blackout options. Learn more about Roller Shades opacity here

    • Cellular Shades Provide excellent insulation and energy efficiency.

    • Solar Shades Highly effective at blocking heat and glare, while still allowing some light to pass through.

    • Roman Shades Elegant and tailored, adding a touch of sophistication.

  • Shutters:

  • Traditions Wood Shutters: can be enhanced with an assortment of paints, stains, options, and upgrades.

  • ​Traditions Composite Shutters are durable and designed to resist fading, cracking, and peeling.

  • 3. Don't Forget About Custom Options

  • Motorization Control your window coverings with the touch of a button or even your voice using smart home technology.



  • Child Safety: You have high standards for safety. So do we.


    The following features on our window treatments have a safety focus:

  • • Cordless lift, motorized lift options, and retractable-cord options replace free-hanging cords that could strangle children or pets.

    • Tension devices keep continuous-loop pull cords taut, eliminating free hanging loops.

  • Unique Materials: Explore options like bamboo, linen, and woven fabrics for a more natural and textured look.
