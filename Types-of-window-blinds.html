Types of Custom Blinds for Your Oklahoma Home

From classic wood blinds to modern aluminum options, we offer a complete range of custom blinds to suit every style and budget in Oklahoma City and Edmond.

Real Wood Blinds

  • • Natural Beauty: Crafted from responsibly-sourced hardwoods with rich textures
  • • Elegance: Adds classic sophistication to living rooms, dining rooms & bedrooms
  • • Energy Efficiency: Provides insulation for Oklahoma's changing seasons

Faux Wood Blinds

  • • Moisture Resistant: Perfect for kitchens, bathrooms & high-humidity areas
  • • Durability: Resists warping, fading, cracking & peeling in Oklahoma climate
  • • Style Options: Solid colors or realistic wood grain patterns available

Composite Wood Blinds

  • • Lightweight: Easier to operate than traditional wood blinds
  • • Coordinated Design: Color palette matches Graber composite shutters for whole-home cohesion
  • • Modern Look: Features wider ladder spacing for contemporary style

Vertical Blinds

  • • Perfect For: Expansive windows and sliding glass doors
  • • Functionality: Vertical slats rotate for light control, draw aside for full access
  • • Material Options: Available in vinyl or fabric with various colors & patterns

Aluminum Blinds

  • • Modern Aesthetic: Sleek appearance perfect for contemporary Oklahoma City homes
  • • Low Maintenance: Resists dust, stains & scratches – easy to clean
  • • Practical Choice: Ideal for kitchens, bathrooms & high-traffic areas

Vinyl Blinds

  • • Budget-Friendly: Affordable window covering solution without sacrificing quality
  • • Easy Care: Simple to clean and maintain
  • • Versatile: Good light control & privacy in a range of colors
