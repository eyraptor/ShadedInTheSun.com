Graber Product Collections

Graber offers an extensive selection of window treatments designed to complement any décor style while delivering exceptional performance. Explore our most popular Graber collections:

Graber Shades

  • • Roller Shades: Clean, modern style in fabrics from sheer to blackout with expanded width options for extra-wide windows.
  • • Solar Shades: Superior sun and heat protection with opacities of 1%, 3%, 5%, and 10% to maintain your view while blocking UV rays.
  • • Cellular (Honeycomb) Shades: Energy-efficient design that reduces heating and cooling costs while offering fabrics from sheer to blackout.
  • • Layered (Zebra) Shades: Modern light control with alternating bands of sheer and opaque fabrics in exclusive prints and patterns.
  • • Roman Shades: Available in Classic, Tailored, and Natural styles with hand-tailored elegance and distinctive designs.
  • • Sheer Shades: Soft fabric vanes that combine gentle light filtering with the functionality of conventional blinds.
  • • Natural Woven Shades: Handwoven from sustainable materials like jute, bamboo, and grasses for casual luxury and eco-friendly style.

Graber Blinds

  • • Wood Blinds (Traditions & Elite): Crafted from responsibly-sourced North American hardwoods milled in Michigan, available in rich finishes and stains.
  • • Faux Wood Blinds: Moisture-resistant composite construction perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, and high-humidity areas.
  • • Aluminum Blinds: Durable, affordable, and available in numerous colors for any room in your home.
  • • Vertical Blinds: Ideal solution for wide windows and sliding glass doors with smooth operation and light control.

Graber Shutters

  • • Interior Wood Shutters: Hand-crafted from 100% North American hardwood for unsurpassed elegance and natural beauty.
  • • Composite Shutters: Engineered material that withstands moisture and high humidity, perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, and laundry rooms.
  • • Custom Styles: Available in a range of louver sizes, panel configurations, and finish options to match your home's architecture.
