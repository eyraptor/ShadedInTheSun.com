Exterior Solar Shades: Built for Oklahoma Outdoor Living

Exterior solar shades bring proven, decades-tested quality to your outdoor spaces. Here's what makes them the right choice for Nichols Hills patios:

Superior Sun Protection

  • • Multiple Openness Choices: Choose Shade Opacities from 1%, 3%, 5%, or 10% openness factors to customize your view and light control. Lower percentages block more light while higher percentages maintain better views.
  • • Energy Efficiency: Block heat before it enters your home through patio doors and windows, reducing cooling costs during Oklahoma summers.
  • • Maintain Your View: Light-filtering materials allow you to see outside while blocking harmful UV rays and reducing glare.

Weather-Resistant Construction

  • • Outdoor-Rated Components: All hardware and materials are specifically designed to withstand outdoor conditions including wind, rain, and temperature fluctuations.
  • • Protective Cassette Housing: Available cassette options protect the fabric roll when retracted, extending the life of your shades.
  • • Stainless Steel Hardware: Rust-resistant components ensure smooth operation and long-lasting durability.

Perfect for Outdoor Entertainment

  • • Daytime Privacy: Enjoy your outdoor space without feeling exposed to neighbors while maintaining airflow and natural light.
  • • Glare Reduction: Watch TV on your patio or work on your laptop outside without annoying screen glare from the sun.
  • • Wind and Mist Protection: Block wind gusts and light rain while still allowing filtered light and air circulation through the fabric.

Motorization for Convenience

  • • AC-Powered Motors: Professional motorization systems operate via wireless remote or wall switch for effortless control.
  • • Control from Anywhere: Lower your shades before stepping outside or adjust them from inside your home for perfect positioning.
  • • Ideal for Large Openings: Motorization makes it easy to operate wide shades covering patios, pergolas, or multi-panel openings.

Custom Fit for Your Space

  • • Wide Width Options: Available up to 12 feet wide to cover large patio openings, pergolas, and outdoor living areas.
  • • Multiple Fabric Colors: Choose from neutral tones that complement your home's exterior.
  • • Professional Installation: Brent and Edna provide expert measurement and installation to ensure perfect fit and smooth operation.
  • • Manual or Motorized Operation: Select the control method that fits your lifestyle and budget, with easy upgrades available.

Custom exterior solar shades from Shaded In The Sun start at $200 per shade, with motorized options typically ranging $200–$400 per shade. Pricing depends on width, opacity, and control type. Every quote includes a free in-home consultation and professional measurement.

Do Patio Shades Really Block Heat in Oklahoma's Summer?

Yes. Exterior solar shades block sunlight before it reaches your patio, reducing heat and glare so outdoor spaces stay comfortable through Oklahoma's hottest afternoons. Opacity levels from 1% to 10% let you balance heat blocking with maintaining your view.

Can Patio Shades Be Motorized?

Yes. Motorization is available on new patio shade installations, not as a retrofit for shades you already own. Motorized patio shades operate via remote or wall switch and are ideal for wide openings covering patios, pergolas, and pool areas.

How Long Does Patio Shade Installation Take?

Most single-opening installations take about 2-3 hours. Larger projects covering multiple patio or pergola openings may take a full day. Every project starts with a free in-home consultation, available evenings and weekends, so we can measure your space beforehand.

What Warranty Comes With Patio Shades?

Patio shades from Shaded In The Sun are backed by comprehensive manufacturer warranties, typically ranging from 1-5 years depending on the specific product. As your authorized dealer, we handle all warranty coordination and support on your behalf.
