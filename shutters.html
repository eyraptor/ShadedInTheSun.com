Why Graber Wood Shutters and Composit Shutters

For unsurpassed elegance and natural beauty, 100% North American hardwood Graber Interior Wood Shutters are a premium window treatment. Hand-crafted in a range of custom styles, real wood shutters are an investment in quality that will add warmth and value to your home. When it comes to composite shutters, Graber Composite Shutters are a versatile solution that offer many of the same benefits as real wood, but in an engineered material that can withstand moisture and high humidity. Composite shutters are perfect for kitchens, laundry rooms, bathrooms, and other high-humidity spaces.
