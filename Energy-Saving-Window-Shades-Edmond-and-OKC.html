The Oklahoma Energy Challenge: Why Your Windows Matter

Oklahoma's climate is unique, throwing everything from intense sunshine to biting cold our way. This constant battle against the elements means our homes work harder to maintain a comfortable indoor temperature. Poorly insulated windows can lead to:

  • • Higher Energy Bills: Heat escaping in winter, heat pouring in during summer.
  • • Uneven Temperatures: Uneven Temperatures: Hot spots near windows, cold drafts elsewhere.
  • • UV Damage: Fading furniture, flooring, and artwork from the sun's harsh rays.

The good news? The right window shades can act as an insulating barrier, tackling these issues head-on. In fact, selecting the right window treatment can significantly cut down on energy loss and boost your home's comfort all year long.

Top Energy-Efficient Window Shades from Shaded In The Sun

When it comes to boosting your home's energy efficiency, not all window treatments are created equal.
Here are some of our top recommendations for Edmond and Oklahoma City homes:

1. Cellular Shades (Honeycomb Shades)

  • • The Powerhouse: These are often considered the champions of energy-saving window treatments. Their unique honeycomb design traps air in pockets, providing an insulating layer that helps keep your home cooler in summer and warmer in winter. They can dramatically reduce heat and air conditioning loss, potentially by as much as 74%!
  • • Ideal For: Any room where temperature control is a priority, from bedrooms to living areas. They're excellent for creating a buffer between your window and living space, reducing heating and cooling energy loss.
  • • Local Benefit: Perfect for managing both the extreme heat and surprising cold snaps we get in Oklahoma City and Edmond.

2. Roller Shades & Solar Shades

  • • Sun Blockers While not as insulating as cellular shades, high-quality solar shades are fantastic for blocking harmful UV rays and reducing solar heat gain. They maintain your view while cutting down on glare and heat. When fully lowered, even traditional blinds can reduce heating and cooling energy loss by up to 40%.
  • • Ideal For: South or west-facing windows where direct sun is an issue. Great for media rooms or areas where you want to protect interiors from fading.
  • • Local Benefit: Essential for managing the intense Oklahoma sun and reducing the need for constant AC use.

3. Roman Shades

  • • Stylish Efficiency: Many Roman shades can be custom-made with thermal linings, creating a substantial barrier against temperature fluctuations. When closed, they provide excellent insulation, helping to conserve energy year-round.
  • • Ideal For: Adding a touch of elegance while improving insulation. They work well in living rooms, dining rooms, and bedrooms.
  • • Local Benefit: A beautiful way to combine aesthetics with practical energy savings for your Edmond home.
