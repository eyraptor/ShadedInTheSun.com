Are Window Treatments Really Worth the Investment?

At Shaded In The Sun here in the Oklahoma City area, I often hear the question: "Are window treatments really worth the money?" It's a valid question, and the short answer is a resounding yes! While they might seem like purely decorative elements, high-quality window treatments offer a multitude of benefits that go far beyond just aesthetics for your Oklahoma City home.


Let's delve into the real value and why investing in the right window coverings can be one of the smartest decisions you make for your property:

Enhanced Energy Efficiency

Oklahoma City summers are scorching, and our winters can bring unexpected chills. Windows are often the biggest source of heat gain in summer and heat loss in winter.

  • • Summer: Block intense sunlight, reducing AC costs
  • • Winter: Extra insulation keeps warm air in
  • • Savings: Energy savings pay for themselves over time

Light Control & Privacy

Complete control over natural light and privacy - from bright and airy to dark and cozy.

  • • Light Filtering: Diffuse harsh sunlight while maintaining brightness
  • • Blackout: Block all light for bedrooms and media rooms
  • • Privacy: Control visibility from outside. Learn about shade opacities

Protecting Your Interiors

The Oklahoma sun can damage furniture, flooring, and artwork. UV rays cause fading and deterioration over time.

  • • UV Protection: Block harmful rays that fade fabrics
  • • Furniture Life: Extend the life of your interior furnishings
  • • Cost Savings: Avoid expensive replacements

Enhanced Aesthetics & Value

Well-chosen window treatments dramatically enhance any room, adding style, sophistication, and personality.

  • • Customization: Wide array of fabrics, colors, and styles
  • • Luxury Feel: High-end treatments elevate perceived value
  • • Resale Value: Significant factor when selling your home

Comfort & Functionality

The right window treatments make your home more comfortable and functional day-to-day.

  • • Noise Reduction: Reduce street noise pollution
  • • Better Insulation: Cozier atmosphere year-round
  • • Motorization: Easy control for hard-to-reach windows

Your Investment in Comfort and Value

For Oklahoma City area homeowners, window treatments are far more than just window coverings. They are a valuable investment that offers significant returns in terms of energy savings, light and privacy control, protection of your belongings, enhanced aesthetics, and overall comfort.


Before you decide, consider your specific needs and the unique climate of Oklahoma City. By choosing the right custom or luxury window treatments, you're not just buying a product; you're investing in the long-term comfort, value, and enjoyment of your home.
