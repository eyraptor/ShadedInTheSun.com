What's the difference between blinds and shades?

Blinds have individual slats that can be tilted to control light and privacy, offering precise light control. They're available in wood, faux wood, and aluminum materials. Shades are made of continuous fabric that raises and lowers, available in various styles including cellular, roller, Roman, and solar shades. Shades typically offer better insulation and softer light filtering, while blinds provide more precise light control and durability.

How do I care for my window treatments?

Maintenance is simple and we provide care instructions for every product. Blinds need regular dusting with microfiber cloth or vacuum brush attachment. Shades require light vacuuming or professional cleaning for delicate fabrics. Shutters need damp cloth cleaning and occasional wood conditioning for hardwood. Motorized treatments need battery charging reminders and system updates.
