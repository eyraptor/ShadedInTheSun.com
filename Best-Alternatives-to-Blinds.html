Best Alternatives to Blinds: Shades & Shutters

Feeling kinda meh about your basic blinds? Did your builder install your blinds?Tired of those little light leaks from the cord holes ruining your vibe? You're not alone! While blinds do the job, sometimes you want window treatments with more personality and better light control. Good news! There are fantastic alternatives to traditional blinds, especially from the quality folks at Graber and Shaded In The Sun. Let's ditch the drab and explore some stylish options.

Here are some top picks from Graber that leave standard blinds in the dust:

Shutters: The Style Champs Think timeless, sturdy, and seriously chic. These add architectural flair instantly.

Why they beat blinds:

  • • Better Light Control: Shutters offer louvers so there are no pesky holes!
  • • Shutters are super durable Shutters resists common issues like warping, cracking, chipping, and fading, even in sunny Oklahoma windows.
  • • They look expensive: Shutters add instant architectural character, giving windows a high-end, custom-finished appearance.

Roman Shades: Soft & Sophisticated Want elegance? Get Roman Shades. They fold up beautifully and offer a high-end, fabric look when down.

Why they beat blinds:

  • • Superior Style & Elegance: Roman shades bring a soft, luxurious feel to a room that hard blinds often lack.
  • • Flawless Light Blocking & Privacy: When you want darkness or complete privacy, Roman shades excel. Unlike many traditional blinds that have small cord holes (route holes) and gaps between slats letting light seep in, a lowered Roman shade is a continuous piece of fabric, providing uniform coverage and eliminating those annoying pinpricks of light.
  • • Added Insulation and Comfort: The fabric construction of Roman shades naturally provides better insulation than thin vinyl or aluminum blind slats. This extra layer can help buffer against Oklahoma's hot summers and chilly winter days, contributing to a more comfortable room and potentially even saving a bit on energy bills.
