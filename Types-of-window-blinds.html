What is the difference between real wood blinds and faux wood blinds?

Real wood blinds are crafted from responsibly-sourced hardwoods, adding natural warmth and elegance to living rooms, dining rooms, and bedrooms. Faux wood blinds are made from moisture-resistant composite materials that resist warping, fading, and cracking — making them ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and high-humidity spaces in Oklahoma homes.

Are faux wood blinds better than real wood for Oklahoma's climate?

For many Oklahoma homeowners, yes. Our humidity and temperature swings can cause real wood to warp over time. Faux wood blinds resist moisture, warping, and fading, making them a reliable long-term choice for Oklahoma City and Edmond homes. They deliver the same warm wood look at a more affordable price.
