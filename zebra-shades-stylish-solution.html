Zebra Shades: A Stylish Solution for Light and Privacy Control

For homeowners seeking both adaptable function and contemporary style, zebra shades emerge as a compelling option. Also known as layered shades or banded shades, these window treatments combine alternating bands of translucent and opaque fabric, giving you precise control over how much light enters a room. When the sheer fabric of one layer aligns with the sheer fabric of the other, light filters through; when the opaque bands overlap instead, the shade softens the light and increases privacy. Zebra shades represent a modern approach to window treatment design that combines practicality with visual appeal, making them an excellent choice for homeowners throughout Oklahoma City and Edmond.

Key Features and Benefits

  • • Privacy and Light Control: Achieve the desired level of privacy and illumination by adjusting the overlap of opaque and sheer bands, or lift the shade entirely for an unobstructed view.
  • • Stylish & On-Trend Fabrics: Zebra shades are available in exclusive prints, patterns, and textures, complemented by a sleek, fabric-wrapped cassette that comes standard.
  • • Modern Control Options: Integrate motorized automation to seamlessly connect with your smart-home system, enabling control via voice, phone, or tablet. Alternatively, opt for the UltraLite Cordless Lift System for effortless operation.
  • • Energy Efficiency: The double-layered fabric construction provides a degree of insulation against both heat and cold.
  • • Easy to Maintain: Designed for lasting performance, layered shades require only occasional light vacuuming with an upholstery attachment to maintain their beauty.

Why Choose Zebra Shades?

  • • Versatile Light Control: Effortlessly adjust the alignment of the stripes to transition between gentle light filtering and full privacy, making them ideal for living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens, and patio doors.
  • • Privacy Management: The innovative dual-layer design ensures privacy without sacrificing natural light. Strategically position the opaque bands to soften light and deter prying eyes while still allowing diffused light to enter through the sheer bands.
  • • Modern and Stylish: Zebra stripes naturally draw attention, making these shades an excellent choice for amplifying color and pattern. Their distinctive striped design can effectively highlight a room's design concept. Custom shades allow you to select fabrics and styles that perfectly complement your home's interior design aesthetic.

Options

  • • Inside or Outside Mount: An inside mount provides a streamlined appearance that integrates seamlessly with your room's design. Conversely, an outside mount positions the shade on the room side, extending wider than the window opening. The choice between inside and outside mount primarily depends on personal aesthetic preference.
  • • Corded: The corded option features a continuous-loop lift system, complete with a tensioner, to eliminate dangling cords and ensure the safety of children and pets.
  • • Cordless: The UltraLite cordless lift system allows for effortless and near-silent raising and lowering of the shade with just a gentle touch.
  • • Motorized: Motorized Zebra Shades can be operated via remote control or through an app on an iPad, utilizing a hub. Enjoy the convenience of controlling your shades from anywhere in the world, seamlessly integrating them into your smart home system.

Not Just Black and White

Zebra shades are often seen in classic dark and light neutrals, but they open up plenty of room for customized design.

  • • Layer with Draperies: Install zebra shades beneath complementary draperies to add pattern and texture.
  • • Add a Cornice: Pair a cornice with zebra shades for a cohesive, tailored window treatment.
  • • Work with a Designer: We help you realize a customized vision that fits your room's décor.

Limitations & Care

Zebra shades are a great fit for most rooms, but a few practical notes are worth knowing.

  • • Not True Blackout: Zebra shades don't offer complete blackout — some light can still filter through. For total darkness, consider other shade options like Roller, Cellular, or Roman Shades.
  • • Built to Last: Whatever fabric you choose, zebra shades are highly durable and provide many years of reliable service.
  • • Simple Maintenance: An occasional light vacuuming with an upholstery attachment is all it takes to keep them looking great.

Is it Right for You?

Zebra shades stand out as both eye-catching and practical.

  • • Bold, Modern Look: Their striped design offers a distinctive touch other shades don't provide.
  • • Easy Operation: The user-friendly roller mechanism makes daily adjustment effortless.
  • • Great Starting Point: If you're looking to set the tone of a room, start with a zebra shade.
