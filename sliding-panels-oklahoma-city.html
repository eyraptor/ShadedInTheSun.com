What Are Sliding Panels or Panel Track Blinds?

Sliding panels are broad fabric panels hung on an aluminum track system, opened and closed with a control wand instead of tilting or stacking like traditional blinds. They're the go-to window treatment for sliding glass doors and other wide openings, and the fabric can range from sheer to blackout depending on what you choose.

Are Panel Tracks a Good Alternative to Vertical Blinds?

Yes. Panel track systems work anywhere vertical blinds are used, but with a cleaner, more modern look and a broader range of uses. The panels retract fully clear of the window when open, and the track system automatically sizes panels wider or narrower based on how many tracks you choose for your space.

Can Panel Track Systems Double as Closet Doors or Room Dividers?

Yes. Beyond windows and sliding glass doors, panel track systems are a popular choice for closet doors and as room dividers in open floor plans, since the same track-and-panel mechanism works just as well indoors as it does over a window.

What Fabric Options Are Available for Sliding Panels?

Sliding panels share fabric collections with roller shades, solar shades, Roman shades, and natural shades, so you can coordinate your panel track system with other window treatments throughout your home. Light control ranges from sheer to blackout depending on which fabric collection you choose.
