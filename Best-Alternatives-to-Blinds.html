What Are People Using Instead of Blinds?

Good news! There are fantastic alternatives to traditional blinds, especially from the quality folks at Shaded In The Sun. Let's ditch the drab and explore some stylish options. Want to understand the value of upgrading your window treatments? We'll show you why it matters.

Here are some top picks from Graber that leave standard blinds in the dust:

Shutters:
The Style Champs

Think timeless, sturdy, and seriously chic. These shutters add architectural flair instantly.

Why they beat blinds:

  • • Better Light Control: Shutters offer louvers so there are no pesky holes!
  • • Shutters are super durable: Shutters resist common issues like warping, cracking, chipping, and fading, even in sunny Oklahoma windows.
  • • They look expensive: Shutters add instant architectural character, giving windows a high-end, custom-finished appearance.

Roman Shades:
Soft & Sophisticated

Want elegance? Get Roman Shades. They fold up beautifully and offer a high-end, fabric look when down.

Why they beat blinds:

  • • Superior Style: A soft, luxurious feel that hard blinds can't match
  • • Flawless Light Blocking: A lowered Roman shade is one continuous piece of fabric — no gaps or cord holes for light to leak through
  • • Added Insulation: Fabric construction buffers against Oklahoma's hot summers and cold winters better than thin blind slats

Cellular Shades:
Energy-Saving Superheroes

These honeycomb-shaped wonders are smart and stylish. Perfect for modern homes.

Why they beat blinds:

  • • Serious Energy Efficiency: Honeycomb pockets trap air for year-round insulation. See energy-saving shades for Oklahoma homes
  • • Seamless Light Control: Uniform filtering or blackout with no slat gaps, plus optional top-down/bottom-up operation
  • • Clean Look & Sound Buffering: Minimal stack when raised, and the air pockets help absorb outside noise

Roller & Solar Shades:
Sleek & Simple

For a clean, minimalist vibe, roller and solar shades are your friend. They roll up neatly out of sight.

Why they beat blinds:

  • • True Blackout (Roller): A solid sheet of coverage with no light gaps — available in true blackout fabric for bedrooms
  • • Heat & Glare Reduction (Solar): Cuts solar heat gain and glare while preserving your view — no closed slats required
  • • UV Protection: Blocks UV rays that fade furniture and flooring, in a wide range of modern fabrics

Panel Track
Blinds

A bonus idea for big windows and doors.

Why they beat blinds:

  • • Built for Large Openings: Wide, sleek panels glide smoothly for sliding glass doors and oversized windows — no tangling like narrow vertical blinds
  • • Room Divider Versatility: Wide fabric panels double as stylish room dividers for open floor plans
  • • Learn More: Read the full sliding panels guide
