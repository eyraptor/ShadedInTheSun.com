Unpacking the Hunter Douglas Price Tag

Before exploring alternatives, let's address the main question. Hunter Douglas's pricing is typically influenced by several key factors:

  • • Aggressive Brand Marketing: Hunter Douglas invests heavily in national advertising and brand recognition. This creates significant overhead that is factored into the final product cost.
  • • Proprietary Innovation & R&D: They are true innovators, developing exclusive, patented systems like PowerView® Automation and unique shade designs. You are paying a premium for this exclusive research and development.
  • • American Assembly: Many of their products are designed and assembled in the U.S.A., which generally involves higher labor costs compared to overseas manufacturing.
  • • Exclusive Dealer Network: Their products are sold through a tightly controlled network of dealers, which helps maintain price integrity and a premium brand experience.

While these points justify the cost for some, we believe you shouldn't have to pay a "brand tax" to get incredible, long-lasting window treatments. That's where brands like Graber and Norman shine.
