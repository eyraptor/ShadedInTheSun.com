Want your home to feel more elevated? Start with your windows. The right shades and blinds do more for a room's look than most people realize — the right fabric, color, and finish can turn an ordinary window into a design statement.

Here's how to bring that kind of luxury into your home.

Luxurious Roller Shade Fabrics

One of the simplest ways to add luxury to a room is with a roller shade in the right fabric.

  • • Fabric: We stock dozens of colors and textures, with semi-sheer materials among the most luxurious
  • • Layer It: Pair with traditional fabric drapery for depth and more light control
  • • Motorized: We install motorized lift systems you control by remote, phone, or app

Read the full roller shades guide →

Richly Hued Natural Shades

Natural shades bring the richness of real materials into your home — no two are exactly alike.

  • • Materials: Wood, bamboo, grasses, and rattan, hand-harvested and sustainable
  • • Color & Texture: Pairs beautifully with whites, off-whites, and sleek materials like silk
  • • Motorized: Available for effortless everyday use — see how these hold up in Oklahoma's climate

Sheer Shades

A Sheer Shade is essentially fabric blinds — soft "slats" of fabric set between two sheer sheets.

  • • Function: Operates like blinds while it filters light in a delicate way
  • • Best For: Rooms where you want serene, diffused light without losing privacy
  • • Fabric Range: We carry a broad selection — there's an option for every room

Real Wood Blinds

For many, the height of luxury is real wood — finely milled and finished to perfection.

  • • Materials: Crafted from North American hardwoods, including sustainably harvested basswood
  • • Finishes: Warm "Honey Maple," a natural finish that shows the grain, or painted tones like light gray and off-white
  • • Best For: Rooms with classic, traditional style that call for real wood character

Roman Shades

We offer Roman shades in flowing, tailored folds — one of the most elegant ways to dress a window.

  • • Fold Styles: Flat pleats, cascading loops, or five tailored styles including balloon and relaxed
  • • Fabric: Premium options including silk and 100% organic Belgian linen
  • • Learn More: See our full Roman shades guide
