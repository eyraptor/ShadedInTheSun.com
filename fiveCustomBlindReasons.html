There are big differences between ready-made and custom-made blinds and shades. Price is the first difference most people notice—custom-made blinds and shades can be more expensive. But that's just one difference. We'll talk about all the differences and explain why custom-made is the right choice for most homeowners.

Better Selection
of Colors and Fabrics

Custom-made blinds and shades offer dramatically more material choices than ready-made options.

  • • Extensive Options: Dozens of cellular, roller, and solar shade fabrics, plus hundreds of drapery options — including moisture-resistant faux wood blinds for kitchens and bathrooms.
  • • Full Color Range: Ready-made collections often carry just a few colors — some only white. Custom opens up a nearly endless palette.
  • • The Right Fit for Your Home: With this many configurations available, you'll find exactly the color and fabric combination your space needs.

Design
Expertise

When you buy custom blinds through Shaded In The Sun, you're benefiting from the experience and knowledge of Edna, your Design Expert.

  • • Guided Selection: Edna helps you navigate hundreds of color, texture, and pattern options without the overwhelm.
  • • Personalized Recommendations: Suggestions tailored to your décor, not a generic best-seller list.
  • • Confidence in Your Choice: Expert guidance means a selection you'll be happy with for years to come.

Expert Advice,
Measurement, and Installation

When you purchase custom blinds and shades from Shaded In The Sun, you get full-service expertise — not just a product.

  • • Design Guidance: Edna helps you choose colors, fabrics, and styles suited to your home.
  • • Professional Measurement & Installation: Brent handles precise measuring and installation, so you don't have to guess.
  • • Optional Motorization: Add motorized window treatment options for extra convenience.

A Perfect Fit

A window treatment that fits perfectly is worry-free and attractive — but ready-made sizing rarely gets you there.

  • • Built to Exact Dimensions: Precise measurements mean minimal light gaps along the sides and smooth, straight operation.
  • • Solves Odd Window Sizes: Ready-made options are built for common sizes only, leaving unusually sized windows underserved.
  • • Professionally Measured and Installed: Every custom window treatment from Shaded In The Sun is precisely measured by Brent and expertly installed for a flawless look.

Unbeatable Quality

Custom-made blinds and shades use higher-quality materials and construction than ready-made alternatives.

  • • Built to Last: Premium components and fabrics outperform and outlast cheaper ready-made options.
  • • Smooth, Quiet Operation: State-of-the-art cordless systems that work reliably for years.
  • • Full-Service Experience: Expert selection assistance plus professional measuring and installation — all included.
