Top Window Treatment Trends

The world of window treatments is constantly evolving, with new styles and innovations emerging every year. Staying current with the latest trends can not only enhance the look and feel of your home, but also improve your lifestyle. As a leading provider of high-quality window treatment solutions, Shaded In The Sun in Oklahoma City and Graber Blinds understand the importance of staying ahead of the curve. Looking for alternatives to traditional blinds? Let's explore five of the hottest window treatment trends currently making waves in Oklahoma City and Edmond.

Natural Textures
and Earth Tones

Woven wood shades crafted from bamboo and reeds are bringing warmth and organic texture back into homes across Oklahoma City and Edmond.

  • • Materials: Bamboo, reeds, and other natural fibers in soft creams to rich browns
  • • Best for: Living rooms and dens where you want a grounded, organic feel
  • • Learn more: Luxury window treatments featuring these natural options

Bold Colors
and Patterns

Bold colors and patterns are replacing bland neutrals in homes across Oklahoma City and Edmond this year.

  • • Styles: Vibrant geometrics, playful florals, and designer fabric prints
  • • Best for: Roller shades — the widest selection of pattern options
  • • Bonus: Add motorization for effortless light control on patterned shades

Smart Home
Integration

Smart home integration is now standard, not a luxury upgrade, for window treatments in Oklahoma City and Edmond.

  • • Control: Smartphone, tablet, or voice control through app-connected motorized shades
  • • Best for: Hard-to-reach windows and multi-window rooms
  • • Bonus: Set schedules so shades adjust automatically throughout the day

Sustainable and
Eco-Conscious Choices

Energy efficiency and eco-conscious materials are top of mind for homeowners upgrading their window treatments this year.

  • • Top pick: Cellular shades trap air in a honeycomb design for year-round insulation
  • • Best for: Lowering summer cooling and winter heating costs
  • • Learn more: Energy-saving window shades for Edmond and OKC homes

Increased Focus
on Privacy

Privacy remains a top priority, with homeowners seeking more nuanced control over light and seclusion.

  • • Top pick: Blackout shades for bedrooms and home theaters
  • • Best for: Top-down/bottom-up shades — privacy below, natural light above
  • • Learn more: Window shade opacities explained

Match Your Trend to a Design Style

Beyond individual product trends, these three interior design styles are shaping Oklahoma City and Edmond homes right now — each pairs naturally with a different window treatment.

Restorative Retreat

Minimalist, wellness-focused rooms built around soft textures and eco-friendly materials. Natural shades bring the wood grain and plant-fiber warmth this style calls for.

Classic Revival

A refined take on mid-century style, built on symmetry, antiques, and rich fabrics. Room-darkening drapery in solid neutrals or traditional patterns completes the look.

Unexpected Optimism

Playful, graphic, and full of color contrast. A layered shade in a bold hue pairs perfectly with this style's disruptive, joyful energy.

Conclusion

Staying abreast of the latest window treatment trends can significantly enhance the look and feel of your Oklahoma City or Edmond home. By incorporating these trends into your window treatment selection, you can create a space that is both stylish and functional. Understanding the value of quality window treatments helps you make informed decisions.
