What Can I Put on My Windows So I Can See Out But Nobody Can See In?

Many Oklahoma homeowners struggle with a common dilemma: they want natural light and the ability to see outdoors, but they also need privacy from neighbors and passersby. Fortunately, modern window shades have evolved to solve this exact problem. The right shade solution lets you enjoy your view while maintaining complete privacy—during the day when you're looking out, and at night when you need darkness.

Privacy Shade Solutions That Work

At Shaded In The Sun, we offer multiple options for homeowners who want to see out but keep prying eyes out. Each solution works differently depending on your specific needs and preferences. Some use dual fabrics, others use intelligent stripe patterns, and some combine positioning with light-control technology.

Zebra Shades

  • • Daytime View: Align sheer bands to look out while light filters in during the day
  • • Evening Privacy: Move opaque bands over sheer bands to block light and reduce visibility
  • • Precise Control: Dual roller system allows quick adjustments to any light level throughout the day

Learn more about Zebra Shades

SmartDrapes (Norman®)

  • • Dual Fabric Design: One drape with both sheer and opaque fabrics built in
  • • Day: Light & View: Keep sheer panel facing out for filtered light and outward visibility
  • • Night: Complete Privacy: Flip or close opaque panel for blackout-level privacy and darkness

Learn more about Norman SmartDrapes

Solar Shades

  • • 99% UV Blocking: Protect furniture and flooring from sun damage
  • • Heat Rejection: Significantly reduce cooling costs during Oklahoma's intense summers
  • • Privacy & View: See outside clearly while making it difficult for outsiders to see in

Discover energy savings with Solar Shades

Bottom-Up/Top-Down
Positioning

  • • Daytime Privacy: Close bottom for eye-level privacy while light flows in from the top
  • • Nighttime Control: Close from top for complete privacy while retaining street-level views if desired
  • • Maximum Flexibility: Works on any shade style: roller, cellular, or solar. Cellular Shades are the most popular choice for bottom-up/top-down installation.

Automate Your Privacy With Motorization

  • • Hands-Free Operation: No need to remember to close shades for privacy
  • • Voice Control: Use Alexa, Google Home, or Apple Siri to adjust privacy instantly
  • • Energy Savings: Schedule solar shades to block heat during the day and open at night
  • • Security: Make your home look occupied when you're away by automating shade patterns

Get expert motorized shade installation

⚠️ Important Note About Nighttime Privacy: Solar shades provide excellent one-way privacy during the day. However, at night when your indoor lights are on, the effect reverses and people outside can see in. If nighttime privacy is your priority, we recommend a Dual Shade system or Zebra Shades.
